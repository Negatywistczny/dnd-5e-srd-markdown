# Coins

Characters often find coins on their adventures i can spend those coins in shops, inns, i other businesses. Coins come in different denominations based on the relative worth of their material. The Coin Values table lists coins i how much they're worth relative to the Złoto Piece, which is the game's main coin. For example, 100 Miedź Pieces are worth 1 Złoto Piece.

A coin weighs about a third of an ounce, so fifty coins weigh a pound.
