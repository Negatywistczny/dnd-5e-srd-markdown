# Crafting Non-Magical Items

**URL źródła:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/equipment:crafting

---

To craft a nonmagical item, you need tools, raw materials, i time, each of which is detailed below. If you meet the requirements, you make the item, i you can use it lub sell it at its normal price.

# Tools

The [Tools](tool.md) section lists which tools are required to make certain items. The DM assigns required tools for items not listed there.

You must use the required tool to make an item i have biegłość w that tool. Anyone who helps you must also have biegłość w it.

# Raw Materials

To make an item, you need raw materials worth half its purchase cost (round down). For example, you need 750 sz of raw materials to make Zbroja płytowa, which sells for 1,500 sz. The DM determines whether appropriate raw materials are available.

# Time

To determine how many days (working 8 hours a day) it takes to make an item, divide its purchase cost in sz by 10 (round a fraction up to a day). For example, you need 5 days to make a Ciężka kusza, which sells for 50 sz.

If an item requires multiple days, the days needn't be consecutive.

Characters can combine their efforts to shorten the crafting time. Divide the time needed to create an item by the number of characters working on it. Normally, only one other character can assist you, but the DM might allow more assistants.
