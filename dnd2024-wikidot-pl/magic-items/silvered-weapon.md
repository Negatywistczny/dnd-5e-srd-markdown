# Silvered Weapon

**URL źródła:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:silvered-weapon

---

Źródło: Podręcznik Mistrza Gry

*Weapon (Any Simple lub Martial), Common*

An alchemical process has bonded silver to this magic weapon. When you score a Trafienie krytyczne with it against a creature that is shape-shifted, the weapon deals one additional die of damage.
