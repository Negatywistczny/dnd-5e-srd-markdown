# Spellfire Spark

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:spellfire-spark

---

Source: Forgotten Realms - Heroes of Faerun

*Origin Feat*

You gain the following benefits.

**Magic Absorption.** Once per turn, when you take damage from a spell or magical effect, you reduce the total damage taken by 1d4. You can’t use this benefit if you have the Incapacitated condition.

**Spellfire Flame.** You learn the [Sacred Flame](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:sacred-flame) cantrip. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell (choose when you select this feat). You can also cast this cantrip as a Bonus Action a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
