# Boon of Skill

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:boon-of-skill

---

Source: Player's Handbook

*Prerequisite: Level 19+*

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of

your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**All-Around Adept.** You gain proficiency in all skills.

**Expertise.** Choose one skill in which you lack Expertise. You gain Expertise in that skill.
