# Spell-Refueling Ring (UA)

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:magic-item-spell-refueling-ring

---

Source: [Artificer UA (17.12.2024)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/the-artificer) and [Eberron Updates UA (27.2.2025)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/eberron-updates)

*Ring, Uncommon (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster)*

While wearing this ring, you can recover one expended spell slot as a Bonus Action. The recovered slot can be of level 3 or lower. Once used, the ring can’t be used again until the next dawn.
