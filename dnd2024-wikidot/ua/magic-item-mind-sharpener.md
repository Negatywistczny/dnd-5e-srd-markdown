# Mind Sharpener (UA)

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:magic-item-mind-sharpener

---

Source: [Artificer UA (17.12.2024)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/the-artificer) and [Eberron Updates UA (27.2.2025)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/eberron-updates)

*Ring, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

The item has 4 charges. When you fail a Constitution saving throw to maintain Concentration, you can take a Reaction and expend 1 of the item’s charges to succeed instead. The item regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn.
