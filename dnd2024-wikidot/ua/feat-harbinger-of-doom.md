# Harbinger of Doom (UA)

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:feat-harbinger-of-doom

---

Source: [UA12 - Villainous Options (02.04.2026)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/posts/2150-designer-insights-from-unearthed-arcana-villainous)

*Path of the Death Knight Feat (Prerequisite: [Death Knight Initiate](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:feat-death-knight-initiate) Feat)*

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength, Constitution, or Charisma score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Ill Omen.** You always have the [Bane](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:bane) spell prepared. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell. You can cast it without expending a spell slot by expending 1 Death Point. You can also cast the spell using any spell slots you have. When you expend Death Points to cast [Bane](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:bane), affected targets subtract 1d6 from attack rolls and saving throws instead of 1d4.
