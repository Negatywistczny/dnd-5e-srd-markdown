# Arcane Restoration (UA)

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:feat-arcane-restoration

---

Source: [UA12 - Villainous Options (02.04.2026)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/posts/2150-designer-insights-from-unearthed-arcana-villainous)

*Path of the Lich Feat (Prerequisite: [Lich Initiate Feat](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:feat-lich-initiate))*

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Essence Rejuvenation.** When you use Soul Siphon to consume a soul, you can choose one or more expended spell slots to recover. The spell slots can have a combined level equal to no more than 4. Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a Short or Long Rest.
