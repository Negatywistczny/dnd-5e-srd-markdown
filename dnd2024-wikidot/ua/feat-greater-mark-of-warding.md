# Greater Mark of Warding (UA)

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:feat-greater-mark-of-warding

---

Source: [UA3 - Eberron Updates (27.02.2025)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/eberron-updates)

*General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Mark of Warding Feat)*

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Warder’s Intuition benefit of your Mark of Warding feat, you can roll 1d6 instead of 1d4.

**Improved Warding.** When you cast [Mage Armor](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:mage-armor) on yourself, you can modify the spell to also affect one willing creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself. Once you modify the spell with this benefit, you can’t do so again until you finish a Long Rest.
