# Returning Weapon (UA)

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:magic-item-returning-weapon

---

Source: [Artificer UA (17.12.2024)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/the-artificer) and [Eberron Updates UA (27.2.2025)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/eberron-updates)

*Weapon (Any Simple or Martial with the Thrown Property), Uncommon*

This magic weapon grants a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it, and it returns to your hand immediately after it is used to make a ranged attack roll.
