# Intellect Fortress (UA)

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:spell-intellect-fortress

---

Source: [UA5 - The Psion 27.05.2025](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/the-psion) and [UA9 - Psion Update (02.10.2025)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/psion-update)

*Level 3 Abjuration (Artificer, Bard, Psion, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** 30 Feet

**Components:** V

**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

For the duration, one willing creature you can see within range has Resistance to Psychic damage, as well as Advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 3.
