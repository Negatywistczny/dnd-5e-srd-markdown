# Mark of Storm (UA)

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:feat-mark-of-storm

---

Source: [UA3 - Eberron Updates (27.02.2025)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/eberron-updates)

*Dragonmark Feat (Prerequisite: Eberron Campaign, Can’t have another Dragonmark Feat)*

You gain the following benefits.

**Windwright’s Intuition.** When you make a Dexterity (Acrobatics) check or an ability check using Navigator’s Tools, you can roll 1d4 and add the number rolled to the ability check.

**Storm’s Boon.** You have Resistance to Lightning damage.

**Storm Magic.** You know the [Thunderclap](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:thunderclap) cantrip. When you reach character level 3, you also always have the [Gust of Wind](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:gust-of-wind) spell prepared. You can cast it once without a spell slot, and you regain the ability to cast it in that way when you finish a Long Rest. You can also cast it using any spell slots you have. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for these spells (choose when you select this feat).

**Spells of the Mark.** If you have the Spellcasting or Pact Magic feature, the spells on the Mark of Storm Spells table are added to that feature’s spell list.
