# Greater Mark of Healing (UA)

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/ua:feat-greater-mark-of-healing

---

Source: [UA3 - Eberron Updates (27.02.2025)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/ua/eberron-updates)

*General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Mark of Healing Feat)*

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Medical Intuition benefit of your Mark of Healing feat, you can roll 1d6 instead of 1d4.

**Improved Healing.** When you cast [Cure Wounds](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:cure-wounds) and roll dice to determine the number of Hit Points restored, you can treat any 1 or 2 on a roll as a 3.
