# Ring of Three Wishes

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:ring-of-three-wishes

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Ring, Legendary*

While wearing this ring, you can expend 1 of its 3 charges to cast [Wish](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:wish) from it. The ring becomes nonmagical when you use the last charge.
