# Potion of Speed

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:potion-of-speed

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Potion, Very Rare*

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [Haste](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:haste) spell for 1 minute (no Concentration required) without suffering the wave of lethargy that typically occurs when the effect ends.

This potion’s yellow fluid is streaked with black and swirls on its own.
