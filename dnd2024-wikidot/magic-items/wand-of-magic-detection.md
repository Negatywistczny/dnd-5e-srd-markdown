# Wand of Magic Detection

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:wand-of-magic-detection

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wand, Uncommon*

This wand has 3 charges. While holding it, you can expend 1 charge to cast [Detect Magic](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:detect-magic) from it. The wand regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn.
