# Hat of Disguise

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:hat-of-disguise

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

While wearing this hat, you can cast the [Disguise Self](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:disguise-self) spell. The spell ends if the hat is removed.
