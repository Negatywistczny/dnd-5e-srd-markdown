# Potion of Mind Reading

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:potion-of-mind-reading

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Potion, Rare*

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [Detect Thoughts](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:detect-thoughts) spell (save DC 13) for 10 minutes (no Concentration required).

This potion’s dense, purple liquid has an ovoid cloud of pink floating in it.
