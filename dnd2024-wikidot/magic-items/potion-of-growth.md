# Potion of Growth

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:potion-of-growth

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Potion, Uncommon*

When you drink this potion, you gain the “enlarge” effect of the [Enlarge/Reduce](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:enlarge-reduce) spell for 10 minutes (no Concentration required).

The red in the potion’s liquid continuously expands from a tiny bead to color the clear liquid around it and then contracts. Shaking the bottle fails to interrupt this process.
