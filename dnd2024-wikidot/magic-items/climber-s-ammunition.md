# Climber's Ammunition

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:climber-s-ammunition

---

Source: DND Beyond Drops - July 2026

*Weapon (any ammunition), Uncommon*

When you hit a solid surface with this piece of magic ammunition, the ammunition attaches to the surface, and a magic rope trails out from behind it. The rope remains in place for 1 hour or until you dismiss it (no action required). It can support up to 500 pounds at once, breaking if that limit is exceeded. Once the rope vanishes, the ammunition becomes nonmagical.

If you hit a creature with this ammunition, the ammunition immediately breaks on impact and deals no damage to the target.
