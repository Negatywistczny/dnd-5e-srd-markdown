# Universal Solvent

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:universal-solvent

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Legendary*

This tube holds milky liquid with a strong alcohol smell. When found, a tube contains 1d6 + 1 ounces.

You can take a Utilize action to pour 1 or more ounces of solvent from the tube onto a surface within reach. Each ounce instantly dissolves up to 1 square foot of adhesive it touches, including [Sovereign Glue](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:sovereign-glue).
