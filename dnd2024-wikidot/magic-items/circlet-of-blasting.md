# Circlet of Blasting

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:circlet-of-blasting

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

While wearing this circlet, you can cast [Scorching Ray](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:scorching-ray) with it (+5 to hit). The circlet can’t cast this spell again until the next dawn.
