# Medallion of Thoughts

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:medallion-of-thoughts

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

The medallion has 5 charges. While wearing it, you can expend 1 charge to cast [Detect Thoughts](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:detect-thoughts) (save DC 13) from it. The medallion regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn.
