# Brooch of Shielding

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:brooch-of-shielding

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

While wearing this brooch, you have Resistance to Force damage, and you have Immunity to damage from the [Magic Missile](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:magic-missile) spell.
