# Cape of the Mountebank

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:cape-of-the-mountebank

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Rare*

This cape smells faintly of brimstone. While wearing it, you can use it to cast [Dimension Door](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:dimension-door) as a Magic action. This property can’t be used again until the next dawn.

When you teleport with that spell, you leave behind a cloud of smoke. The space you left is Lightly Obscured by that smoke until the end of your next turn.
