# Crystal Ball of Mind Reading

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:crystal-ball-of-mind-reading

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Legendary (Requires Attunement)*

While touching this crystal orb, you can cast [Scrying](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:scrying) (save DC 17) with it. In addition, you can cast [Detect Thoughts](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:detect-thoughts) (save DC 17) targeting creatures you can see within 30 feet of the spell’s sensor. You don’t need to concentrate on this *Detect Thoughts* spell to maintain it during its duration, but it ends if the *Scrying* spell ends.
