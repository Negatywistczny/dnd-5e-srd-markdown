# Potion of Poison

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:potion-of-poison

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Potion, Uncommon*

This concoction looks, smells, and tastes like a [Potion of Healing](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:potion-of-healing) or another beneficial potion. However, it is actually poison masked by illusion magic. [Identify](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:identify) reveals its true nature.

If you drink this potion, you take 4d6 Poison damage and must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have the Poisoned condition for 1 hour.
