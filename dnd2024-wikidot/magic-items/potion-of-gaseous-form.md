# Potion of Gaseous Form

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:potion-of-gaseous-form

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Potion, Rare*

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [Gaseous Form](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:gaseous-form) spell for 1 hour (no Concentration required) or until you end the effect as a Bonus Action.

This potion’s container seems to hold fog that moves and pours like water.
