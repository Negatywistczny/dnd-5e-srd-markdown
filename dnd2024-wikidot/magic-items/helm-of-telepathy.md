# Helm of Telepathy

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:helm-of-telepathy

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

While wearing this helm, you have telepathy with a range of 30 feet, and you can cast [Detect Thoughts](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:detect-thoughts) or [Suggestion](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:suggestion) (save DC 13) from the helm. Once either spell is cast from the helm, that spell can’t be cast from it again until the next dawn.
