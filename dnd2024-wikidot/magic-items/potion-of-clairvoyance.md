# Potion of Clairvoyance

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:potion-of-clairvoyance

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Potion, Rare*

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [Clairvoyance](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:clairvoyance) spell (no Concentration required).

An eyeball bobs in this potion’s yellowish liquid but vanishes when the potion is opened.
