# Stormwalker's Cloak

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:stormwalker-s-cloak

---

Source: DND Beyond Drops - July 2026

*Wondrous Item, Rare (requires Attunement)*

While wearing this cloak, you gain the following benefits.

**Shocking Feedback.** The cloak has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. You can expend 1 charge to cast [Hellish Rebuke](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:hellish-rebuke) from the cloak (DC 13), and the spell deals your choice of Lightning or Thunder damage instead of the usual Fire damage.

**Storm Resistance.** You have Resistance to Lightning and Thunder damage.
