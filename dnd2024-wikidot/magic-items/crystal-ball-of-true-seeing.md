# Crystal Ball of True Seeing

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:crystal-ball-of-true-seeing

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Legendary (Requires Attunement)*

While touching this crystal orb, you can cast [Scrying](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:scrying) (save DC 17) with it. In addition, you have Truesight with a range of 120 feet centered on the spell’s sensor.
