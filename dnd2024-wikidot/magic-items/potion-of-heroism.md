# Potion of Heroism

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:potion-of-heroism

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Potion, Rare*

When you drink this potion, you gain 10 Temporary Hit Points that last for 1 hour. For the same duration, you are under the effect of the [Bless](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:bless) spell (no Concentration required).

This potion’s blue liquid bubbles and steams as if boiling.
