# Rod of Resurrection

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:rod-of-resurrection

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Rod, Legendary (Requires Attunement)*

The rod has 5 charges. While you hold it, you can cast one of the following spells from it: [Heal](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:heal) (expends 1 charge) or [Resurrection](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:resurrection) (expends 5 charges).

The rod regains 1 expended charge daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll 1d20. On a 1, the rod disappears in a harmless burst of radiance.
