# Ring of Animal Influence

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:ring-of-animal-influence

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Ring, Rare*

This ring has 3 charges, and it regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, you can expend 1 charge to cast one of the following spells (save DC 13) from it:

- [Animal Friendship](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:animal-friendship)
- [Fear](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:fear) (affects Beasts only)
- [Speak with Animals](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:speak-with-animals)
