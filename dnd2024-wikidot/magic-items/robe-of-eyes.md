# Robe of Eyes

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:robe-of-eyes

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement)*

This robe is adorned with eyelike patterns. While you wear the robe, you gain the following benefits:

**All-Around Vision.** The robe gives you Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.

**Special Senses.** You have Darkvision and Truesight, both with a range of 120 feet.

**Drawbacks.** A [Light](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:light) spell cast on the robe or a [Daylight](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:daylight) spell cast within 5 feet of the robe gives you the Blinded condition for 1 minute. At the end of each of your turns, you make a Constitution saving throw (DC 11 for *Light* or DC 15 for *Daylight*), ending the condition on yourself on a success.
