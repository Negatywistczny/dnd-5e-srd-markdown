# Talisman of the Sphere

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:talisman-of-the-sphere

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Wondrous Item, Legendary (Requires Attunement)*

While holding or wearing this talisman, you have Advantage on any Intelligence (Arcana) check you make to control a [Sphere of Annihilation](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:sphere-of-annihilation). In addition, when you start your turn in control of a *Sphere of Annihilation*, you can take a Magic action to move it 10 feet plus a number of additional feet equal to 10 times your Intelligence modifier. This movement doesn’t have to be in a straight line.
