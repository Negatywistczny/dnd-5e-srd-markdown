# Ring of Jumping

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:ring-of-jumping

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Ring, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

While wearing this ring, you can cast [Jump](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:jump) from it, but can target only yourself when you do so.
