# Sylvan Talon

**Source URL:** http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/magic-item:sylvan-talon

---

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

*Weapon (Dagger, Rapier, Scimitar, Shortsword, Sickle, or Spear), Common (Requires Attunement)*

While this weapon is on your person, you understand the nonwritten communication of all Fey, and they understand yours.

**Secret Message.** As a Magic action, you can use the weapon to cast [Message](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:message). Once this property is used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
